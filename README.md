# 🚀 SkillBridge AI

### Your Personal AI Career Mentor

SkillBridge AI is an AI-powered career guidance platform designed to help students, freshers, and aspiring professionals make smarter career decisions.

Instead of searching across multiple websites, students can receive personalized career guidance, skill-gap analysis, learning roadmaps, interview preparation, project recommendations, resume feedback, and scholarship assistance—all in one place.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of students struggle with:

* Lack of proper career guidance
* Confusion about what to learn next
* No personalized roadmap
* Poor interview preparation
* Limited access to mentorship
* Difficulty identifying skill gaps

As a result, many students waste time learning the wrong skills or following unstructured paths.

---

## 💡 Solution

SkillBridge AI acts as a virtual career mentor that helps students:

✅ Identify missing skills

✅ Generate personalized learning roadmaps

✅ Practice interviews

✅ Improve resumes

✅ Discover career opportunities

✅ Track career progress

All powered by AI.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🧭 Career Counselor

Get personalized career guidance for any domain.

### 📊 Skill Gap Analyzer

Analyze current skills and identify missing competencies.

### 🗺️ Learning Roadmap Generator

Generate structured learning plans and career roadmaps.

### 💡 Project Recommender

Discover beginner, intermediate, and advanced projects.

### 🎤 AI Interview Coach

Practice technical and HR interview questions.

### 📄 Resume Reviewer

Receive resume analysis and improvement suggestions.

### 🎓 Scholarship Finder

Explore scholarships and educational opportunities.

### 📈 Career Dashboard

Track progress and learning journey.

### 🕒 Search History

View and revisit previous AI-generated guidance.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. User selects a module.
2. User enters career goals or queries.
3. Gemini AI processes the request.
4. SkillBridge AI generates personalized recommendations.
5. User can save and revisit previous searches.
